History

Arch Linux was founded by Judd Vinet in March 2002. It's known for its simplicity, modernity, pragmatism, user centrality, and versatility.

Features

  • Rolling releases
  • A package management system called Pacman
  • Highly customizable

Arch Linux is designed to be simple, minimalist, and lightweight, adhering to the KISS principle. It's perfect for users who want to build their system from the ground up.

Community

Arch Linux has a strong and active community which maintains comprehensive documentation and provides support via forums and IRC.
